# Carnage on Seve's Last Stand — R3 Draft

## Overview

Alex Baker posted the round of the day with 42 Stableford points to vault from joint third into clear second in the Trophy. Stuart Neumann's 37 was the lowest return, but every card carried at least one quadruple bogey or worse. John Patterson's Trophy lead survived but was cut from ten shots to three; David Mullin held the Green Jacket lead at exactly seven over Jon Baker; Neumann remained anchored to the Wooden Spoon.

## The Round

Royal Óbidos, on its third TEG visit, is Seve Ballesteros' final design. It bit all six players.

**Jon Baker** raced out with 16 points through the first five — par, birdie at the par-5 2nd, par, bogey, par — then took the worst of the 10–12 stretch: a double-bogey 6 at the 10th, a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 11th, and a double-bogey 6 at the 12th. Eight shots dropped in three holes. He finished on 34 points, his front nine markedly stronger than his back.

**John Patterson** bled eight shots across holes 2–4: a double-bogey 7 at the par-5 2nd, a triple-bogey 6 at the par-3 3rd, and a triple-bogey 7 at the 4th. Further damage came with a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 12th and a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 18th. He signed for 34 points — enough to keep the Trophy lead, not enough to keep the cushion.

**Alex Baker** posted the day's best round, 42 points, despite his own wreckage: a triple-bogey 7 at the 10th, a triple-bogey 8 at the par-5 11th and a double-bogey 6 at the 12th (eight shots gone across 10–12), plus a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 14th.

**David Mullin** carded 33 points. His low point was a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 10th.

**Gregg Williams** put together 38 points, the second-best return of the day, with a quadruple-bogey 7 at the par-3 15th the main blemish.

**Stuart Neumann** managed 37. Quadruple-bogey 8s at the par-4 4th and the par-4 16th bookended the card, but in between he produced 9 points across holes 6–8 (par, bogey, par at the par-3 6th, par-5 7th and par-3 8th) and 12 points across 10–13 (bogey, par, bogey, par). He closed 8-6-7 — eight shots dropped across 16–18.

## Race Shifts

**Trophy:** Patterson's ten-shot lead is now three. Alex Baker's 42 lifted him past Mullin into clear second. Mullin slipped from second to third and is now level with Jon Baker on 110.

**Green Jacket:** Mullin's lead over Jon Baker stays at exactly seven — Mullin's quintuple-bogey 9 at the 10th was offset by Baker's quadruple-bogey 9 at the 11th. Patterson is third, Williams fourth.

**Wooden Spoon:** Neumann stays last and the gap to Williams above him grew from 14 to 15, despite his 37-point return.
