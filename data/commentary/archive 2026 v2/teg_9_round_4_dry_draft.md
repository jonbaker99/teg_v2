# Round 4 Draft — Praia D'El Rey

## Overview

Alex Baker posted the round of the day with a personal-best 44 Stableford points (gross +27). Stuart Neumann was furthest adrift, dropping shots in heavy stretches at the start and finish. John Patterson saw a 3-point overnight Trophy lead extended to 6 to win TEG 9; David Mullin stretched his Green Jacket lead from 7 to 10 to take the Jacket; Neumann finished the Wooden Spoon by 16.

## The Round

**Alex Baker** played the best round of the day. His Trophy push survived an early quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 6th — part of a rocky front-nine stretch (triple at the 4th, bogey at the 5th, quad at the 6th, bogey at the 7th) — which he closed out with a birdie 2 at the par-3 8th. He then climbed through the middle of the round to draw level with Patterson for the Trophy lead at the par-3 14th, where even a double-bogey 5 was enough to pull alongside. The lead lasted one hole. Across 14–17 Baker dropped 11 shots: double bogey at the 14th, quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 15th, triple-bogey 7 at the 16th, double-bogey 7 at the par-5 17th. He still signed for 44 points, a personal best.

**John Patterson** carded a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 5th but otherwise did enough. The 3-point cushion he brought into the round absorbed the blow-up, and when Baker faltered at 15–17 the Trophy was decided. Patterson wins TEG 9 by 6.

**David Mullin** opened with a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 1st. He steadied with four pars at 2–5 before a double-bogey 6 at the 5th, then strung three pars together at the par-5 7th, par-3 8th and par-4 9th for 9 points across the stretch. The Jacket lead, 7 overnight, was 10 by the close.

**Jon Baker** was the only realistic threat to Mullin and made no inroads. A quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 17th ended any lingering hope. He finishes second on gross, 10 back.

**Gregg Williams** ran up an 11 at the par-5 7th — a sextuple bogey — the day's biggest single-hole number. He finishes fifth in the Trophy.

**Stuart Neumann** dropped 9 shots across 2–5 (double, triple, double, double). He rallied through the middle with 16 points across 11–15, including a birdie at the par-4 13th and pars at the 11th, 12th and 14th. The finish undid him: quadruple-bogey 8 at the 16th, double-bogey 7 at the 17th, double-bogey 6 at the 18th, for 8 shots dropped over the last three.

## Race Results

**Trophy:** Patterson takes TEG 9 by 6 points over Alex Baker, having entered the round 3 clear. Baker briefly drew level at the 14th — the closest anyone got to Patterson all tournament — before the 14–17 collapse settled it.

**Green Jacket:** Mullin wins by 10 from Jon Baker, extending the gap from 7 despite the opening quad at the 1st. Jon Baker's quad at the 17th ended the contest.

**Wooden Spoon:** Neumann finishes 16 behind Williams. The gap was 15 overnight and was never seriously threatened.
