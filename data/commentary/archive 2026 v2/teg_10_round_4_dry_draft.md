# Round 4 — Boavista

## Overview
Alex Baker shot 51 Stableford points, the best round in TEG history to date, while John Patterson posted 21 points for the worst card of the day. Baker turned a tie at the top into an 11-point Trophy win over Gregg Williams; David Mullin sealed the Green Jacket by 12 over Jon Baker; Patterson confirmed the Wooden Spoon by 32.

## The Round

The day began with Alex Baker and Gregg Williams tied for the Trophy on 133. Williams struck first, reaching the par-4 5th, where a birdie 3 drew him level at the top of the leaderboard for the only time on the day. That birdie also stopped a small bleed — a double-bogey 7 at the par-5 3rd and a bogey 4 at the par-3 4th.

From that point the round belonged to Alex Baker. Across holes 5 to 10 he assembled 21 Stableford points: bogey at the par-4 5th (3 pts), par at the par-3 6th (4), par at the par-4 7th (4), bogey at the par-5 8th (3), par at the par-4 9th (3), par at the par-4 10th (4). He finished with 51 points for the round, gross 17 over par.

Williams could not stay with him. At the par-3 6th he ran up a quadruple-bogey 7. Holes 12 to 14 then cost him six shots — double bogey 6, double bogey 7, double bogey 5. He rallied with 9 points across 15-17 (bogey, par, par), before a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 18th closed the round.

David Mullin played quietly and effectively, taking his Green Jacket lead from 10 strokes to 12. Jon Baker matched Williams' 9-point burst across the same 15-17 stretch (bogey, par, par) to secure second in the Jacket race, 12 behind Mullin.

Stuart Neumann lost eight shots over the opening three holes — double bogey at the 1st, a quadruple-bogey 7 at the par-3 2nd, and a double-bogey 7 at the par-5 3rd — and did not recover.

John Patterson posted his worst round to date at 21 points. The card included a sextuple-bogey 11 at the par-5 8th, a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 10th, and a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 18th.

## Race Results

**Trophy:** Alex Baker wins on 184, 11 points clear of Gregg Williams. The two had started the round level on 133; Baker's 51 to Williams' 40 settled the contest.

**Green Jacket:** David Mullin wins on +51 gross, 12 clear of Jon Baker. Mullin's 10-shot overnight lead grew by two more across the round.

**Wooden Spoon:** John Patterson claims it on 112 points, 32 behind Stuart Neumann. A 14-point deficit became a 32-point gap after his 21-point round.
