# Deal Or No Deal: Mullin's Kentish Coronation

## OVERVIEW

David Mullin won the Trophy on 154 Stableford points, two clear of Alex Baker, and also took the Green Jacket at +68 gross, seven shots ahead of Jon Baker. Jon Baker collected the Wooden Spoon on 125 points.

## Deal Me In: Mullin Lays Down a Marker

Round 1 was played at Royal Cinque Ports, Deal. Mullin posted 40 points and led both competitions by the close. The round's defining stretch was his run from the 9th to the 12th — par, birdie (a 3 at the par-4 10th), par, par — for 13 Stableford points in four holes. He took outright lead of the Trophy at the par-5 16th.

Alex Baker's day was defined by two blowups: a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 11th, and a quintuple-bogey 10 at the par-5 16th. Gregg Williams ran up a 9 at the par-5 3rd. Jon Baker dropped to last in the Wooden Spoon race at the par-3 4th with a bogey 4, and would not move off the bottom for the rest of the week.

## Littlestone, Big Lead: Mullin Out to Nine

Round 2 was at Littlestone on the Romney Marshes. Mullin posted 41 points and extended his Trophy lead to nine.

Alex Baker bled twelve shots across holes 2 to 5: triple bogey at the par-4 2nd, double at the par-4 3rd, triple at the par-4 4th, quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 5th. He added a further quadruple — an 8 at the par-4 8th — later in the round.

Mullin's own round included a triple at the par-4 4th and a double at the par-5 5th, before he stopped that slide with a birdie at the par-5 7th. He repeated the trick to close, recovering from bogey-bogey-double at 15, 16 and 17 with a birdie 4 at the par-5 18th.

## Prince's and the Pauper Field: Mullin Cruises

Round 3 was at Prince's on the Shore/Dunes routing. Mullin won the day with 36 points despite a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 8th. He was markedly stronger on the back nine — a ten-point split between halves — and signed off with 13 points across holes 15 to 18: pars at 15, 16 and 17 (the par-3) and a birdie 3 at the par-4 18th.

Alex Baker ran up an 8 at the par-4 10th. Jon Baker put together a tidy middle stretch but it ended with a triple bogey at the par-3 17th. Gregg Williams piled up nine points across holes 9 to 11 with three straight pars, then ran up a triple-bogey 8 at the par-5 12th.

Mullin's Trophy lead stood at eight overnight; the Jacket lead remained twelve.

## Sixteenth Time Unlucky: Alex Baker's 10 Hands Mullin the Trophy

Round 4 returned to Prince's. Alex Baker came out hot with three pars at holes 1, 2 and 3 for ten points, and added eleven more across holes 13 to 15 with three further pars. With the Trophy gap shrinking, he then ran up a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 16th — the same hole number on which he had taken a 10 in Round 1 at Deal. The contest was over.

Mullin's own round included a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 10th but he still posted 37 points. Jon Baker had the most striking stretch of his week — birdie at the par-5 12th, birdie at the par-4 13th, then pars at 14 and 15 for 14 points in four holes — before closing with a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 18th.

## HOW THE COMPETITIONS WERE DECIDED

**Trophy (Stableford).** Mullin led at every checkpoint: by 3 after R1, 9 after R2, 8 after R3 and 2 at the finish. He drew level on the par-4 11th in R1 and took outright lead at the par-5 16th — the decisive takeover — built on his 13-point burst from the 9th. His halfway lead of nine survived a quiet R3 and was eroded in R4 by Alex Baker's opening ten-point stretch and his eleven-point middle stretch. The 10 at the 16th in R4 ended the chase. Final margin: two points.

**Green Jacket (Gross).** Mullin led by 5 after R1, 12 after R2, 12 after R3 and 7 at the finish. Alex Baker drew level at the par-4 6th in R1; Mullin drew level back at the par-4 9th, and from there the lead only grew. Quadruples at the par-5 8th in R3 and the par-4 10th in R4 did not threaten the margin. Mullin won at +68, seven clear.

**Wooden Spoon.** Jon Baker dropped to last at the par-3 4th of Round 1 with a single bogey, and remained 4th at every checkpoint thereafter. The R3 collapse at the par-3 17th and the closing 8 at the par-4 18th of R4 confirmed the position. Final score: 125 points.

## PLAYERS

- **David Mullin** — Wire-to-wire double winner; led both competitions at every checkpoint, weathered the quadruples at the 8th in R3 and 10th in R4, and finished with his third best Trophy result.
- **Alex Baker** — Runner-up in the Trophy by two; five quadruple-or-worse holes including matching 10s at the 16th in R1 (Deal) and R4 (Prince's).
- **Jon Baker** — Bottom from R1 H4 to the final putt; the week's most thrilling stretch (14 points across holes 12-15 at Prince's on Sunday) bracketed by the quad at the 13th in R3 and the 8 at the 18th in R4.
- **Gregg Williams** — Mid-pack; capable of three pars in a row at holes 9-11 in R3, also responsible for a 9 at the par-5 3rd at Deal and a triple at the par-5 12th at Prince's.
