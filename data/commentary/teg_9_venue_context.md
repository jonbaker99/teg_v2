# TEG 9 - Venue context

**Location:** Lisbon Coast, Portugal (2016) - TEG's 3rd visit to Lisbon Coast, Portugal

### Round 1: Royal Óbidos  (the 2nd TEG round at this venue)
- Seve Ballesteros' final course design, a challenging championship layout with dramatic water features. Hosts European Challenge Tour events.
- _Parkland, Seve Ballesteros, Obidos, Lisbon Coast, Portugal_

### Round 2: Praia D'El Rey  (the 3rd TEG round at this venue)
- A spectacular course mixing parkland and links golf, with the back nine featuring dramatic coastal dunes. Highly ranked in European and world golf.
- _Links and Parkland, Cabell B. Robinson, Obidos, Lisbon Coast, Portugal_
