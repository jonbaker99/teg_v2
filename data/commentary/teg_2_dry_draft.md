# OVERVIEW

David Mullin won the Trophy at +6 net vs par, by 7 from Jon Baker. Mullin also took the Green Jacket at +66 gross, by 11 from Stuart Neumann. Henry Meller collected the Wooden Spoon at +39 net vs par. TEG 2 was played over three days at Boavista Golf & Spa Resort, Lagos — TEG's first visit to the Algarve.

# ROUND 1 — Mullin Sets a Marker, Boavista Sets a Trap

Boavista was new to the field, and Mullin set the standard immediately. His +0 net (+20 gross) was both the best net round and the best gross round in TEG history to date (b01, b02). Jon Baker's +4 net (+32 gross) was the second-best net round in TEG history to date and the third-best gross round (b05, b12). Stuart Neumann's +11 net (+29 gross) ranked as the third-best net round and the second-best gross round in TEG history to date (b13, b06).

After early bunching at the top, Mullin led the Trophy by 4 at the end of the round and led the Jacket by 9.

The back nine — and the par-3 14th in particular — wrecked the rest of the field. Gregg Williams made a 9 (sextuple bogey) at the 14th (b15) inside a 12-to-18 stretch in which he dropped 22 shots: double bogey at the par-4 12th, double at the par-5 13th, the 9 at the 14th, double at the par-4 15th, triple at the par-5 16th, a 9 (quintuple bogey) at the par-4 17th, and double at the par-4 18th (b19).

Henry Meller dropped 20 shots across 11-15: triple at the par-3 11th, quadruple at the par-4 12th, quadruple at the par-5 13th, an 8 (quintuple bogey) at the par-3 14th, and quadruple at the par-4 15th (b21, b20). The Wooden Spoon position changed hands once in the whole tournament — at R1 H3, where Meller dropped to the bottom and stayed there (b49).

# ROUND 2 — Baker's Day, Mullin's Stumble

Baker posted +1 net (+29 gross), the second-best net round in TEG history to date (b07). Neumann's +27 gross was the second-best gross round in TEG history to date (b08). Mullin's +28 gross was the third-best gross round in TEG history to date (b11).

The Trophy lead changed hands within the round. Baker took it outright at R2 H4 with a par at the par-3 4th (b45), and built on it with 10 Stableford points across holes 6-8: bogey at the par-3 6th, par at the par-4 7th, bogey at the par-5 8th (b46). Mullin reclaimed the lead outright at R2 H5 with a bogey at the par-4 5th (b43), before Baker drew level again at H6.

Mullin then bled 10 shots across 16-18: triple at the par-5 16th, triple at the par-4 17th, and an 8 (quadruple bogey) at the par-4 18th (b48). Baker, however, also collapsed at the close — 13 shots dropped across 15-18: triple at the par-4 15th, double at the par-5 16th, an 8 (quadruple) at the par-4 17th, an 8 (quadruple) at the par-4 18th (b42). Baker finished the day with the Trophy lead of 3 over Mullin. The Jacket lead never moved: Mullin led by 8.

Meller dropped 11 shots across 13-16: double at the par-5 13th, triple at the par-3 14th, triple at the par-4 15th, triple at the par-5 16th (b50).

# ROUND 3 — Mullin's Masterpiece

Mullin closed with -2 net (+18 gross). Both were records: the best net round and the best gross round in TEG history to date (b03, b04).

Baker dropped 12 shots out of the gate across holes 1-5: double at the par-4 1st, double at the par-3 2nd, triple at the par-5 3rd, triple at the par-3 4th, double at the par-4 5th (b40). Mullin retook the Trophy lead outright at R3 H3 with a bogey at the par-5 3rd (b16) — the decisive takeover of the tournament — and was not headed again.

Neumann broke a run of bogey-or-worse with a birdie at the par-3 11th, after bogey at the par-5 8th, double at the par-4 9th and bogey at the par-4 10th (b41).

Meller's +20 gross was the second-best gross round in TEG history to date (b09) — by which point he was long since anchored to the Spoon.

# HOW THE COMPETITIONS WERE DECIDED

**Trophy (Net VP).** Mullin led after R1 by 4. Baker took over after R2 by 3, having edged ahead at R2 H4 and survived Mullin's late collapse at 16-18. Mullin reclaimed the lead outright at R3 H3 with a bogey at the par-5 3rd (b16), and Baker's 12-shot bleed across the opening five holes ended the contest. Final margin: 7, at +6 to Baker's +13.

**Green Jacket (Gross).** Mullin led from R1 H4 onwards and was never headed. Round-by-round leads of 9, 8 and 11. He won by 11 at +66 from Neumann (b14).

**Wooden Spoon.** Meller dropped to the bottom at R1 H3 (b49), the only Spoon position change of the tournament. R1 holes 11-15 (20 shots dropped) and R2 holes 13-16 (11 shots dropped) settled it. His record-equalling +20 gross in R3 came too late. Final Spoon total: +39 net vs par (b22).
