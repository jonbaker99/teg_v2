# Dry Storyline Draft — TEG 9, Lisbon Coast (2016)

## OVERVIEW

John Patterson won the Trophy on 168 Stableford points, six clear of Alex Baker — an all-time TEG Stableford record. David Mullin won the Green Jacket at +67 Gross, ten clear of Jon Baker — an all-time TEG Gross record. Stuart Neumann took the Wooden Spoon on 127 pts.

## Round 1 — Seve's Last Design Bares Its Teeth

Royal Óbidos, the second TEG visit to Seve Ballesteros' final design.

The Trophy lead changed hands repeatedly through the early holes: Jon Baker drew level at the 2nd, Mullin drew level at the 3rd, Gregg Williams drew level at the 4th, Patterson moved outright in front at the 5th, and Jon Baker drew level again at the 6th. Jon Baker ended the round as Trophy leader on 39 pts, one clear of Patterson on 38.

Patterson's card was disfigured by a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 4th (+6). He stayed in touch regardless.

Alex Baker recorded the round's biggest single number: a septuple-bogey 11 at the par-4 14th (+7). His Gross +43 was his worst TEG round to date.

In the Gross competition, Mullin moved outright in front at the 3rd, was drawn level by Jon Baker at the 6th, moved back to a share at the 15th, and took the outright Jacket lead for keeps on the 18th. He signed for +15, leading Jon Baker by 2.

Neumann fell to the bottom of the Trophy at the 11th and confirmed it at the 15th, anchored by a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 4th and a quintuple-bogey 8 at the par-3 15th. Round score: 23 pts, last.

## Round 2 — Patterson, 49, and Goodnight

Praia D'El Rey, third TEG visit; parkland front, coastal-dunes back.

Patterson took outright Trophy lead at the 1st. Mullin drew level at the 2nd. Patterson then moved clear again at the 11th and was not caught for the rest of the tournament.

Patterson posted 49 points — the second-best round in TEG history to date — for a Gross +14. The scoring spine was holes 9–13, worth 17 points: bogey at the par-4 9th (3), par at the par-5 10th (4), par at the par-3 11th (3), birdie at the par-5 12th (4), par at the par-4 13th (3). He added 14 more points across the closing four: par-bogey-par-bogey at 15–18 (4-3-4-3).

He took a 10-point Trophy lead into Round 3.

Alex Baker posted a personal-best 44 points and a personal-best Gross +27 on the same card, despite a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 10th and a quadruple-bogey 7 at the par-3 14th.

Gregg Williams ran up a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 9th.

Mullin signed for Gross +14, extending the Jacket lead to 7.

## Round 3 — Back to Royal Óbidos, Back to the Grind

Royal Óbidos again, third TEG visit.

Patterson's 34 points was his worst round of the week. The Trophy lead shrank from 10 to 3 but he stayed in front.

Mullin ran up a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 10th. He still signed for Gross +22, and the Jacket lead held at 7.

Neumann improved to 37 points but remained 6th on the Trophy.

## Round 4 — Patterson Signs for the Record

Praia D'El Rey, fourth TEG visit.

Patterson closed with 47 points to finish on 168 — an all-time TEG Stableford record — winning the Trophy by 6 from Alex Baker. His card included a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 5th. His Gross total of +88 was a personal best.

Mullin opened with a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 1st and still signed for Gross +16. Final Gross total +67, an all-time TEG record, winning the Jacket by 10 from Jon Baker.

Alex Baker matched his Round 2 personal-best: 44 points and Gross +27, despite a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 6th and a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 15th. Tournament Gross +127 was a personal best. He drew level with Jon Baker at the 14th in the Trophy chase before finishing runner-up.

Gregg Williams ran up a sextuple-bogey 11 at the par-5 7th. His tournament Gross +115 was nonetheless a personal best.

Jon Baker had a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 17th. His tournament Gross +77 was a personal best.

Neumann posted 37 points again, including a five-hole stretch worth 16 points at holes 11–15: par at the par-3 11th (3), par at the par-5 12th (3), birdie at the par-4 13th (4), par at the par-3 14th (3), bogey at the par-4 15th (3). Final Trophy total 127 pts, last.

## HOW THE COMPETITIONS WERE DECIDED

**The Trophy.** Jon Baker led after Round 1 on 39 pts, one clear of Patterson. Patterson took outright lead on the 1st hole of Round 2 at Praia D'El Rey and never gave it back. His 49 points that day opened a 10-point gap. The lead narrowed to 3 after a 34 at Royal Óbidos in Round 3, then stretched back to 6 with a closing 47 at Praia D'El Rey. Final: Patterson 168, Alex Baker 162. Decisive moment: the 1st hole of Round 2.

**The Green Jacket.** Mullin took the outright Gross lead on the 18th green of Round 1 at Royal Óbidos and held it for the remaining 54 holes. Round-by-round Gross: +15, +14, +22, +16. Lead by round: 2, 7, 7, 10. Final: Mullin +67, Jon Baker +77, margin 10. Decisive moment: the 18th of Round 1.

**The Wooden Spoon.** Neumann dropped to the bottom of the Trophy on the 11th of Round 1 and confirmed it on the 15th of Round 1. He was 6th in every round. Round scores: 23, 30, 37, 37. Final: 127 pts. Decisive moment: the 15th of Round 1.
