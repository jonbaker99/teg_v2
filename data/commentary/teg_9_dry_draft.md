# OVERVIEW

John Patterson won the Trophy on 168 Stableford points, by 6 from Alex Baker — the highest Trophy total in TEG history. David Mullin won the Green Jacket at +67 Gross, by 10 from Jon Baker — his seventh Jacket. Stuart Neumann took the Wooden Spoon on 127 points.

# ROUND 1 — Carnage at Óbidos: Mullin sets the record, Baker sets the other one

Round 1 at Royal Óbidos, Seve Ballesteros's final design and its third TEG visit. Two course records fell on the same day. David Mullin posted 87 gross (+15) to break the prior Royal Óbidos course record of 90, set across the previous six visits. At the other end, Alex Baker signed for 115 gross (+43), a new course-worst, exceeding the prior worst of 108.

Blow-ups were spread across the field. John Patterson took a 10 (sextuple bogey) at the par-4 4th, the SI 1. Gregg Williams ran up a 9 at the par-4 1st and another 9 at the par-3 15th. Alex Baker had an 11 at the par-4 14th. Stuart Neumann had a 9 at the 4th and an 8 at the par-3 15th.

The Trophy lead changed hands repeatedly through the opening holes — Jon Baker on the 2nd, Mullin on the 3rd, Williams on the 4th, Patterson taking it outright on the 5th, Jon Baker back in front on the 6th. Jon Baker led the Trophy at the close by 1, Patterson second. In the Jacket, Mullin moved to outright lead on the 18th and held a 2-shot cushion. Neumann dropped to last outright at the 15th and stayed there for the rest of the tournament. Patterson signed for 38 points; Mullin's round-Gross was +15.

# ROUND 2 — Forty-Nine and Out of Sight

Round 2 at Praia D'El Rey, the Cabell Robinson parkland-links hybrid. John Patterson posted 49 Stableford points (+14 gross) — the second-best round in TEG history to date.

Patterson took the Trophy lead outright at the 1st. Mullin drew level on the 2nd. Patterson then piled up 17 points across holes 9 to 13: a bogey 5 at the par-4 9th (3 pts), a par 5 at the par-5 10th (4 pts), a par 3 at the par-3 11th (3 pts), a birdie 4 at the par-5 12th (4 pts), and a par 4 at the par-4 13th (3 pts). He moved back in front on the 11th. He closed with 14 points across holes 15-18: par at the par-4 15th (SI 3), bogey at the 16th, par at the par-5 17th (SI 1, the hardest hole) and bogey at the 18th. By the end of the round he led the Trophy by 10.

Alex Baker posted a personal-best 44 points and a personal-best Gross round of +27 on the same course — a 100 gross, his Praia D'El Rey course PB by 9 shots over his TEG 7 visit. Gregg Williams ran up a 10 at the par-4 9th. Mullin extended his Jacket lead to 7.

# ROUND 3 — Patterson Wobbles, Lead Holds

Round 3, back at Royal Óbidos. Patterson signed for 34 points after three quadruple bogeys: an 8 at the par-4 12th (SI 2), and a 9 at the par-5 18th. His Trophy lead was cut from 10 to 3.

Jon Baker opened with 16 points across holes 1-5 — par at the 1st, birdie 4 at the par-5 2nd, par at the par-3 3rd, bogey at the par-4 4th (SI 1) and par at the par-5 5th — but the charge faded. Mullin had his own blow-up, a 9 at the par-4 10th, but held the Jacket lead at 7. Williams and Neumann added more damage.

# ROUND 4 — Patterson Closes Out, Baker Climbs to Second

Round 4 at Praia D'El Rey. Patterson signed for 47 points to win the Trophy by 6, with a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 5th (SI 2) the only significant blemish.

Alex Baker matched his earlier personal best with another 44 points and another +27 Gross round, leapfrogging into second place on the Trophy. Mullin took a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 1st but still posted +16 Gross to extend his Jacket lead to 10. Williams ran up an 11 at the par-5 7th. Neumann assembled 16 points across holes 11-15 — par at the par-3 11th, par at the par-5 12th, birdie 3 at the par-4 13th, par at the par-3 14th (SI 17, the easiest hole) and bogey at the par-4 15th (SI 3) — but the Spoon was long settled. Alex Baker drew level with Jon Baker on Trophy points at the 14th before moving clear.

# HOW THE COMPETITIONS WERE DECIDED

**Trophy.** Jon Baker led by 1 after Round 1. Patterson took outright lead on the 1st hole of Round 2 at Praia D'El Rey, and his 49-point round opened a 10-point gap by the close — the decisive moment. A 34-point Round 3 cut the margin to 3; a 47-point Round 4 restored it to 6. Final margin: 6 points over Alex Baker. Patterson's 168 is the highest Trophy total in TEG history.

**Green Jacket.** Mullin led the Gross from the 18th hole of Round 1 at Royal Óbidos, the moment he closed out his course-record 87. His lead by round ran 2, 7, 7, 10. Final margin: 10 over Jon Baker, who posted a personal-best Gross total of +77. Mullin's seventh Jacket, third in a row.

**Wooden Spoon.** Neumann dropped to last outright at the par-3 15th in Round 1 at Royal Óbidos — an 8 — and remained bottom for all 56 remaining holes. Round scores of 23, 30, 37, 37 left him 41 points adrift of safety. Back-to-back Spoons.

# PLAYERS

- **John Patterson** — Trophy winner on 168 (record). Scrappy R1 38 with a 10 at the SI 1 4th; 49 at Praia D'El Rey in R2; 34 in R3 with quadruples at the 12th and 18th; 47 to close. Personal-best Gross total of +88.
- **David Mullin** — Green Jacket winner at +67, by 10. Wire-to-wire from R1 H18. Course record 87 at Royal Óbidos in R1. Visible blemishes were a 9 at the 10th in R3 and an 8 at the 1st in R4.
- **Alex Baker** — Trophy runner-up. R1 course-worst 115 at Royal Óbidos, then personal-best 44s in R2 and R4 (each with a personal-best +27 Gross round). Personal-best Gross total of +127. Course PBs at both venues despite an 11 at the 14th in R1 and four quadruple bogeys across the week.
- **Jon Baker** — Jacket runner-up for the fourth time in five TEGs on a personal-best Gross total of +77. Briefly led the Trophy on R1 H6; 16-point opening five in R3.
- **Gregg Williams** — Defending Trophy champion. Three sextuple bogeys: 9 at the 1st in R1, 10 at the 9th in R2, 11 at the 7th in R4. Personal-best Gross total of +115.
- **Stuart Neumann** — Wooden Spoon on 127, back-to-back. Bottom from R1 H15 onwards. Round scores 23, 30, 37, 37. Five quadruple-or-worse holes across the week. Late 16-point burst (holes 11-15) in R4.
