# Round 3 — Quinta da Marinha — Draft

## Overview
Jon Baker posted the round of the day in relative terms, taking the Green Jacket lead off David Mullin and closing six shots on Williams in the Trophy. Stuart Neumann posted the worst, bleeding 17 shots over the opening five holes and an 11 at the 10th to blow the Wooden Spoon gap out to 22. Mullin's 26 points was his worst TEG round to date and cost him the Jacket.

## The Round

**Gregg WILLIAMS** carried an 11-shot Trophy cushion into the round and gave six of it back. The damage came in a four-hole stretch from the 8th through the 11th: a triple-bogey 7 at the par-4 8th, a double-bogey 5 at the par-3 9th, a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 10th and a quintuple-bogey 10 at the par-5 11th — 14 shots dropped in four holes. A further quadruple-bogey 8 followed at the par-4 13th. He still leads the Trophy, but by five rather than eleven.

**Jon BAKER** avoided the headline disasters and was the round's quiet beneficiary. He drew level with Mullin for the Green Jacket lead at the 12th with a par 4, and finished the day two clear on Gross and five behind Williams in the Trophy.

**David MULLIN** began the day as Green Jacket leader, six clear on Gross. He posted 26 Stableford points — his worst TEG round to date — with a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 12th the hole on which the Jacket lead changed hands. He drew level again with Baker at the par-4 15th (par 4) but couldn't hold it, and ended the round two adrift.

**Alex BAKER** had a grim middle stretch. Three triple bogeys in a row at the 3rd, 4th and 5th cost nine shots. The par-4 8th brought a sextuple-bogey 10. The par-4 12th brought a quadruple-bogey 8. The card's only oasis was a birdie 3 at the par-4 17th, which broke a long run of bogey-or-worse.

**Henry MELLER** stayed clear of the Trophy and Jacket conversations and bookended his card with quadruple bogeys: an 8 at the par-4 13th and an 8 at the par-4 18th.

**Stuart NEUMANN** bled 17 shots across the opening five holes — a quintuple-bogey 10 at the par-5 1st, a double-bogey 5 at the par-3 2nd, a double-bogey 7 at the par-5 3rd, a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 4th and a triple-bogey 6 at the par-3 5th. The back nine brought a quintuple-bogey 8 at the par-3 9th and a sextuple-bogey 11 at the par-5 10th.

## Race Shifts

**Trophy:** Williams' overnight lead of 11 over Jon Baker is down to 5. Baker closed six shots without posting any blow-ups of his own; Williams gave them away on the 8–11 stretch and the 13th. The race is live going into the final round.

**Green Jacket:** Lead change. Mullin began the round six clear on Gross; Baker drew level at the 12th as Mullin made quadruple-bogey there, Mullin drew level back at the 15th, and Baker emerged two ahead by the close.

**Wooden Spoon:** Neumann was eight adrift overnight; the opening 10, the 9 at the 4th, the 8 at the 9th and the 11 at the 10th have stretched the gap to 22.
