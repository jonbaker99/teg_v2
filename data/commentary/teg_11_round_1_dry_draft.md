# Round 1 Draft — Catalan Carnage

## Overview
Jon Baker posted the day's best card with 35 Stableford points to lead both the Trophy and Green Jacket after Round 1 at PGA Catalunya's Stadium Course. John Patterson posted the worst, 26 points, after three quad-or-worse holes and a 12–14 stretch that bled 11 shots. Jon Baker leads the Trophy by 3 over Alex Baker and the Jacket by 2 over David Mullin; Patterson sits 3 adrift at the foot of the Wooden Spoon.

## The Round
TEG's second visit to the Stadium Course — Spain's premier championship test, designed for the European Tour by Angel Gallardo and Neil Coles — produced the carnage the venue is built for.

Gregg Williams opened by bleeding eight shots across the first three holes: triple-bogey 7 at the par-4 1st, triple-bogey 7 at the par-4 2nd, double-bogey 7 at the par-5 3rd. John Patterson was alongside him in trouble across the same stretch, double-bogeying the 2nd and 3rd before running up a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 4th — three holes for nine shots dropped.

Inside the front nine the leaderboard moved repeatedly. At the par-5 3rd, Jon Baker's par drew him level for both the Trophy and Green Jacket. David Mullin's bogey at the same hole pulled him level in the Trophy. At the par-4 6th, Mullin's par drew him level in both races. At the par-5 7th, Patterson's bogey briefly drew him level for the Trophy lead. Williams then added a quintuple-bogey 8 at the par-3 5th.

Patterson's back nine was where the round was lost. He ran up a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 10th, then a 12–14 stretch that dropped 11 shots: triple-bogey 8 at the par-5 12th, quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 13th, triple-bogey 7 at the par-4 14th. The triple at the 14th dropped him to the bottom of the Wooden Spoon race. A quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 17th followed.

Alex Baker's round was the steadier of the chasers, undermined by a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 13th; he finished on 32 points. Mullin closed on 29 points and gross +20. Williams, despite the opening eight-shot bleed and the quintuple at the 5th, salvaged 30 points. Jon Baker stayed out of the worst of it and signed for 35 points, gross +18.

## Race Shifts
The Trophy lead changed hands four times inside the first seven holes — Jon Baker level at the 3rd, Mullin level at the 3rd and 6th, Patterson level at the 7th — before Jon Baker pulled clear to lead Alex Baker by 3 and the rest by 5 or more. In the Green Jacket, Jon Baker drew level at the 3rd, Mullin joined him at the 6th, and Jon Baker edged ahead by round's end on gross +18, two clear of Mullin and ten clear of the chasing pack. Patterson dropped to the bottom of the Wooden Spoon at the 14th and finished 3 adrift of Mullin.
