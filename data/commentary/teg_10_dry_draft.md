# TEG 10 — Algarve, 2017 — Dry Storyline Draft

## 1. Overview

Alex Baker won the Trophy on 184 Stableford points, beating Gregg Williams by 11 — both the score and the margin are TEG records. David Mullin won the Green Jacket at +51 gross, 12 clear of Jon Baker. John Patterson took the Wooden Spoon on 112 points.

## 2. Round 1 — Mullin Lays Down a 49 — and a Marker

The tournament opened at Boavista (Howard Swan parkland, the 11th TEG round at the venue). David Mullin posted 49 Stableford points at +4 gross — the second-best round in TEG history to date — to lead the Trophy by 8 and the Gross from the front. Stuart Neumann finished the round with a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 18th. John Patterson dropped to the bottom of the field at the 4th hole and went on to a quintuple-bogey 10 at the par-5 8th.

## 3. Round 2 — Palmares Bows In — and Williams Posts 48

TEG's first visit to Palmares used the Praia/Alvor combination (Robert Trent Jones Jr., links-meets-parkland). Gregg Williams posted a personal-best 48 points at +14 gross. Alex Baker also posted a personal best, 45 points at +23 gross, cutting Mullin's lead from 8 to 5. Mullin shot 11 over par for the round and retained the Gross lead.

John Patterson opened with a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 1st. Stuart Neumann bled 13 shots across three holes — a quintuple-bogey 10 at the par-5 13th, a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 14th and a triple-bogey 7 at the par-4 15th — and then ran up a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 18th.

## 4. Round 3 — Baker Takes the Wheel on the Alvor/Lagos Loop

Palmares again, this time on the Alvor/Lagos combination — also a TEG debut, more parkland in character. Alex Baker posted a personal-best 47 points at +22 gross and took the outright Trophy lead with a par at the 6th. Gregg Williams briefly took the outright lead at the par-5 13th after Baker ran up a quintuple-bogey 10 at the same hole. Baker drew level again at the 15th and led from there.

John Patterson bled 19 shots across holes 11–15: triple-bogey 7 at the par-4 11th (+3), quintuple-bogey 8 at the par-3 12th (+5), triple-bogey 8 at the par-5 13th (+3), quadruple-bogey 7 at the par-3 14th (+4) and quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 15th (+4). He also ran up a quintuple-bogey 10 at the par-5 8th. Mullin shot 23 over par for the round and retained the Gross lead.

## 5. Round 4 — 51: The Best Round in TEG History

Back at Boavista (the 12th TEG round there). Alex Baker closed with 51 Stableford points at +17 gross — the best round in TEG history to date — to win the Trophy by 11 on 184. Gregg Williams drew level at the Trophy summit at the par-4 5th before Baker's round pulled away. Mullin shot 13 over par to secure the Green Jacket by 12.

John Patterson posted 21 points at +40 gross, a personal worst, including a sextuple-bogey 11 at the par-5 8th. He finished the tournament bottom of the field on 112 points.

## 6. How the Competitions Were Decided

**The Trophy (Stableford).** Mullin led after R1 by 8, after R2 by 5. Alex Baker took the outright lead at the 6th hole of R3 (Palmares Alvor/Lagos) — the decisive takeover — was briefly overhauled by Gregg Williams at the 13th of the same round, drew level again at the 15th, and led for the rest of the tournament. Williams drew level once more at R4 hole 5 before Baker's 51 settled it. Final margin: 11 points.

**The Green Jacket (Gross).** Mullin led from R1 hole 6 onwards (Jon Baker briefly drew level at R1 hole 9, the only other lead change). Round scores of +4, +11, +23, +13 produced a final +51 and a 12-shot win over Jon Baker.

**The Wooden Spoon.** Stuart Neumann was bottom after hole 1 of R1; John Patterson took over at hole 4 of R1 and was bottom at every subsequent checkpoint. Round scores of 29, 35, 27, 21 produced a final 112 points.

## 7. Players

- **Alex Baker** — 2nd after R1 (8 back), 2nd after R2 (5 back), 1st after R3, 1st after R4. Personal-best rounds of 45 (R2) and 47 (R3); a record 51 in R4; TEG-record 184 total; Trophy winner by 11.
- **David Mullin** — Round scores of 49, then +11, +23, +13 gross. Led the Gross wire-to-wire; second-best round in TEG history (49) in R1; Green Jacket by 12.
- **Gregg Williams** — Personal-best 48 in R2; briefly outright Trophy leader at R3 hole 13; drew level again at R4 hole 5; runner-up on the Trophy, 11 adrift.
- **Jon Baker** — Briefly outright Trophy leader at R1 hole 9 and drew level on the Gross at R1 hole 9; runner-up in the Green Jacket, 12 back.
- **Stuart Neumann** — Sextuple-bogey 10s at Boavista's 18th (R1) and Palmares' 18th (R2); 13 shots dropped across holes 13–15 of R2.
- **John Patterson** — Bottom from R1 hole 4 to the finish. 10 at the par-5 8th (R1), 10 at the par-4 1st (R2), 10 at the par-5 8th and 8 at the par-3 12th (R3), 11 at the par-5 8th (R4). 21-point closing round, a personal worst. Wooden Spoon on 112.
