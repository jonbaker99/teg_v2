# TEG 18 — Dry Storyline Draft

## OVERVIEW

Alex Baker won the Trophy on 169 Stableford points, eight clear of John Patterson. Gregg Williams won the Green Jacket at +66 gross, 14 clear of David Mullin. Jon Baker took the Wooden Spoon on 126 Stableford points.

## Round 1 — 46 Points and a 9 at the Last: The Alex Baker Method Arrives

All four rounds were at Camiral (PGA Catalunya); R1 and R2 on the Tour course, R3 and R4 on the Stadium. R1 was on the Tour.

Alex Baker took outright Trophy lead at H3, briefly ceded it to David Mullin at H4, and retook it at H5. He never gave it back. His card included a quadruple-bogey 7 at the par-3 2nd and a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 18th, but he piled up 17 points across holes 8-12 (bogey-bogey-par-par-bogey) and another 13 across holes 14-16 (par, birdie at the par-5 15th, par at the par-3 16th). He posted 46 points to lead by 12.

Around him, the Tour course was punishing. David Mullin made a sextuple-bogey 11 at the par-5 15th, which dropped him to the bottom of the Spoon race. John Patterson bled 22 shots across holes 3-10 (quad, triple, double, double, triple, triple, triple, double). Gregg Williams made a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 7th and a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 17th, with a birdie at the 15th breaking a long bogey-or-worse run.

Jon Baker took outright Jacket lead during the round and led by 2 at the close.

## Round 2 — Alex Makes 11 at the Last and Still Leads by 11

Another Tour-course round.

Alex Baker carded a sextuple-bogey 11 at the par-5 18th and a quintuple-bogey 8 at the par-3 16th, with a birdie at the par-4 17th sandwiched between them. He still posted 41 points and led the Trophy by 11.

Jon Baker took outright Jacket lead at H9 (a par at the par-4 9th) and finished the inward run par-par-par-par-par from H14 (birdie at the par-3 14th, then pars at 15, 16, 17, 18) for 16 points across those five holes. He led the Jacket by 2 at the end of the day.

The Spoon changed hands four times in the round. Gregg Williams dropped to bottom at H2; Jon Baker took it at H3 and again at H7; David Mullin took it at H18 (a double-bogey 7 at the par-5 18th) and ended the day there. Mullin had also bled 11 shots across holes 4-6 (quad, triple, quad). Patterson bled 11 shots across holes 6-10 (triple, then four straight doubles).

## Round 3 — One Hole, Two Trophies: The Stadium's Second

The pivot, on the Stadium course.

At the par-4 2nd, Gregg Williams made par to take outright Jacket lead — a lead he never relinquished. At the same hole, Jon Baker made a triple-bogey 7 and dropped to the bottom of the Spoon, where he stayed for the rest of the tournament.

Gregg followed with 12 points across holes 2-5 (par-par-par-par on a par-4, par-5, par-4, par-3), before a double-bogey 6 at the par-4 6th ended the run. Jon Baker made a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 4th.

Alex Baker bled 12 shots across holes 6-10 (triple, double, double, double, triple) but then piled up 21 points across holes 11-16: par at the par-3 11th, bogey at the par-5 12th, par at the par-4 13th, par at the par-4 14th, bogey at the par-5 15th, bogey at the par-3 16th. He posted 44 points to extend his Trophy lead to 12. David Mullin gained 10 points across holes 8-10 (par, bogey, birdie at the par-4 10th).

Gregg ended the day leading the Jacket by 8. Jon Baker was bottom of the Spoon.

## Round 4 — Gregg Closes Out the Stadium; Alex Closes Out the Trophy

The Stadium again.

Gregg Williams posted 12 on the Stadium gross-vs-par and won the Jacket by 14. His round included 9 points across holes 8-10 (par at the par-3 8th, bogey at the par-4 9th, par at the par-4 10th).

Alex Baker carded a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 14th and a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 17th, posted 38 points, and won the Trophy by 8.

Jon Baker bled 10 shots across holes 7-11 (five straight double bogeys), broke the streak with a birdie at the par-4 13th, and finished bottom for the round on 29 points to confirm the Spoon. John Patterson piled up 9 points across holes 4-6 (bogey, bogey, par) and made a birdie at the par-4 17th after a triple-double-bogey run from H14.

## HOW THE COMPETITIONS WERE DECIDED

**The Trophy (Stableford).** Alex Baker took outright lead at R1 H5 and was leader at the end of every round (12, 11, 12, 8). Round scores 46-41-44-38 for 169. The decisive moment was R1 H5; the only question after that was the margin. Final margin: 8 over John Patterson.

**The Green Jacket (Gross).** Jon Baker led after R1 (by 2) and R2 (by 2). Gregg Williams took outright lead at R3 H2 — the tenth and final lead change — and led by 8 at the end of R3 and by 14 at the end of R4. Round gross-vs-par 27-14-13-12. Final margin: 14 over David Mullin.

**The Wooden Spoon.** Bottom of the race passed through Mullin, Patterson, Williams, Mullin and Jon Baker in R1, then Williams, Jon Baker (twice) and Mullin in R2. Jon Baker took final possession at R3 H2 (triple-bogey 7 at the par-4 2nd) and stayed bottom through R3 and R4. Round scores 30-39-28-29 for 126.

## PLAYERS

- **John Patterson** — Trophy runner-up, 8 behind. Bled 22 shots across holes 3-10 in R1 and 11 shots across holes 6-10 in R2.
