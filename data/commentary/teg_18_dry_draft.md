# TEG 18 — Dry Storyline Draft

## OVERVIEW
Alex Baker won the Trophy on 169 Stableford points, by 8 from John Patterson (161). Gregg Williams won the Green Jacket at +66 gross, by 14 from David Mullin. Jon Baker took the Wooden Spoon on 126 Stableford points. All four rounds were played at Camiral Golf & Wellness (PGA Catalunya): R1–R2 on the Tour course, R3–R4 on the Stadium course.

## ROUND 1 — 46 and Gone: Baker Lays Down the Law at the Tour
Played on the Tour course.

Alex Baker posted 46 Stableford points. The round included a quadruple-bogey 7 at the par-3 2nd and a quadruple-bogey 9 at the par-5 18th, but the scoring was built on a 17-point burst across holes 8–12: bogey at the par-3 8th, bogey at the par-4 9th, par at the par-5 10th, par at the par-3 11th, bogey at the par-4 12th.

John Patterson dropped 22 shots across holes 3–10: quadruple bogey at the par-4 3rd (8), triple bogey at the par-4 4th, double bogey at the par-5 5th, double bogey at the par-4 6th, triple bogey at the par-5 7th, triple bogey at the par-3 8th, triple bogey at the par-4 9th, double bogey at the par-5 10th.

David Mullin ran up an 11 (sextuple bogey) at the par-5 15th and a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 6th. The 15th dropped him to the bottom of the Wooden Spoon race.

Gregg Williams posted quadruple bogeys at the par-5 7th (9) and the par-4 17th (8).

By the close: Alex Baker led the Trophy by 12. Jon Baker led the Gross at +2, with Williams third. Jon Baker was bottom of the Spoon.

## ROUND 2 — The Eleven That Didn't Matter
Played on the Tour course.

Alex Baker signed for 41 points despite a quintuple-bogey 8 at the par-3 16th and a sextuple-bogey 11 at the par-5 18th. He broke the run with a birdie at the par-4 17th between those two holes.

Jon Baker closed with 16 points across holes 14–18: birdie at the par-3 14th, par at the par-5 15th, par at the par-3 16th, par at the par-4 17th, par at the par-5 18th. Earlier in the round he had dropped to the bottom of the Spoon race at the 3rd (double bogey) and again at the 7th (triple bogey). David Mullin took the bottom slot back at the 18th.

Gregg Williams went 13 points across holes 13–16 (four straight pars) and added 9 points across holes 3–5.

John Patterson lost 11 shots across holes 6–10 (triple, double, double, double, double).

End of round: Alex Baker led the Trophy by 11. Jon Baker still led the Gross by 2. David Mullin was bottom of the Spoon.

## ROUND 3 — Stadium Day: Williams Pounces, Jon Baker Slips
Played on the Stadium course.

At the par-4 2nd, Gregg Williams parred to take outright lead of the Green Jacket. On the same hole, Jon Baker made triple bogey (7) and dropped to the bottom of the Spoon race.

Williams then went 12 points across holes 2–5 (par, par, par, par at par 4–5–4–3) before a double bogey at the par-4 6th broke the run. He posted 13 for the round.

Jon Baker made a quintuple-bogey 9 at the par-4 4th.

Alex Baker dropped 12 shots across holes 6–10: triple bogey at the par-4 6th, double bogey at the par-5 7th, double bogey at the par-3 8th, double bogey at the par-4 9th, triple bogey at the par-4 10th. He then took 21 points from holes 11–16: par at the par-3 11th, bogey at the par-5 12th, par at the par-4 13th, par at the par-4 14th, bogey at the par-5 15th, bogey at the par-3 16th. He signed for 44.

End of round: Alex Baker led the Trophy by 12. Williams led the Gross by 8. Jon Baker was bottom of the Spoon.

## ROUND 4 — Coronation at Camiral
Played on the Stadium course.

Gregg Williams shot +12 gross for the round. He went 9 points across holes 8–10 (par, bogey, par).

Alex Baker made a sextuple-bogey 10 at the par-4 14th and a quadruple-bogey 8 at the par-4 17th, and still signed for 38 points.

Jon Baker dropped 10 shots across holes 7–11 (five consecutive double bogeys at the par-5 7th, par-3 8th, par-4 9th, par-4 10th, par-3 11th). He broke an eleven-hole bogey-or-worse streak with a birdie at the par-4 13th. He posted 29 for the round.

John Patterson broke his own bogey-or-worse streak with a birdie at the par-4 17th.

## HOW THE COMPETITIONS WERE DECIDED

**Trophy (Stableford).** Alex Baker took outright lead at the 5th hole of R1 (after a brief David Mullin cameo at the 4th) and led wire-to-wire: by 12, 11, 12 and 8 across the four rounds. His 46-point opener was the decisive move. Round scores: 46, 41, 44, 38 = 169. John Patterson finished second on 161. Margin: 8 points.

**Green Jacket (Gross).** Jon Baker led after R1 (+2) and R2 (+2). Gregg Williams took outright lead at the 2nd hole of R3 on the Stadium course and was never headed. Williams's rounds went 27, 14, 13, 12 over par; the Stadium 13 and 12 turned a two-shot deficit into a 14-shot final margin. Final: Williams +66, runner-up David Mullin.

**Wooden Spoon.** Jon Baker was bottom after R1, was relieved of the position by David Mullin during R2, and reclaimed it at the 2nd hole of R3 (triple bogey on the Stadium opener). He stayed bottom through R4. Round scores: 30, 39, 28, 29 = 126.
